Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about booking, payments, and travel.

You can book directly through our website, via WhatsApp, or by calling our team. Simply choose your preferred tour, select your dates, and complete the booking form. Our team will confirm availability and send you a detailed itinerary within 24 hours.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), bank transfers, and cash payments at our offices. For online bookings, we use secure payment gateways to protect your information. Payment plans are available for select packages.

Free cancellation is available up to 48 hours before your tour for most packages. Cancellations within 48 hours may incur a 50% charge. No-shows are non-refundable. Some special packages or peak season bookings may have different terms - these will be clearly stated at booking.
