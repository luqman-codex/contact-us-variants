About the Role

We're looking for a passionate Customer Success Specialist to join our Dubai team. You'll be the first point of contact for travelers planning their dream vacations across the UAE and Turkey.

In this role, you'll help customers navigate our tour offerings, answer questions via WhatsApp, email, and phone, and ensure every traveler has an exceptional experience from booking to return.

What You'll Do

  • Respond to customer inquiries via WhatsApp, email, and phone within 5 minutes during business hours
  • Guide customers through tour selection, booking process, and payment
  • Handle booking modifications, cancellations, and refund requests professionally
  • Coordinate with tour operators to ensure smooth customer experiences
  • Follow up with customers post-tour to collect reviews and feedback
  • Maintain accurate records in our CRM system

What We're Looking For

  • 1-3 years of experience in customer service, travel, or hospitality
  • Excellent written and verbal communication in English (Arabic, German, or Turkish is a plus)
  • Strong problem-solving skills and ability to handle difficult situations calmly
  • Proficiency with CRM tools, WhatsApp Business, and basic computer skills
  • Passion for travel and genuine interest in helping people
  • Flexibility to work occasional evenings/weekends during peak seasons

Nice to Have

Tourism Degree UAE Experience Multilingual Sales Background Viator/GetYourGuide
